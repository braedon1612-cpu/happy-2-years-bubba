A Letter To My Bubba ❤️

❤️ Happy 2 Year Anniversary ❤️


Two years ago today, on 7 June at Texas American, I asked you to be my girlfriend, my other half, and the most important person in my life. That moment changed everything for me, and if I had the chance to do it all over again, I would choose you every single time.

Over these past two years, you have shown me what true love really looks like. Thank you for being honest, trustworthy, loyal, and respectful. Those aren't just words with you, they are the qualities that make you the incredible woman you are. You have always supported me, stood by me through everything, and loved me even when things weren't easy.

I know this past year hasn't always been perfect. I've made mistakes, and I know I've hurt you at times. Looking back, this has taught me one of the most important lessons in life: Never take advantage of or abuse the good things and the people you are blessed with.

I am truly sorry for the times I let you down, and I want you to know that I never take your love, patience, and commitment for granted.

Through it all, you've remained by my side, and that means more to me than words can ever express. You are more than just my girlfriend. You are my peace, my happiness, my safe place, and my person.

You bring so much love, strength, and joy into my life, and I genuinely couldn't have asked for a better partner.

Thank you for every memory we've shared, every laugh, every lesson, every hug, and every moment of love. I cherish all of it.

Here's to the beautiful two years behind us and to all the amazing memories still to come. No matter what, my love for you remains strong, and I look forward to continuing this journey together.

❤️🥰😘🤗

I love you so much.

Happy 2 Year Anniversary, my love. ❤️

Forever Yours,

Braedon ❤️
